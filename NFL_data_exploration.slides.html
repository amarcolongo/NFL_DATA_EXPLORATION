Right away we see that we have a problem with the data in the career_stats_qb dataframe. This dataframe contains data for everyone that has ever thrown a pass in an NFL game. Teams sometimes run trick plays where runningbacks or wide recievers throw pass, it also contains data for players that have had very short NFL careers for whatever reason and that could badly skew our data. I will need to set a cutoff point for passing attempts and remove any below that point. This will weed out the problematic data.¶

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

My dataset is made up of 8525 rows and 19 columns.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am really interested in looking at the effects of different factors on QB success. I really like how this dataset has data for so many different factors.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I think that the way that the dataset has grouped all the different variables into columns will be of great help when it comes to analyzing the data.
